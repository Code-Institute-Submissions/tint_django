TRANSCRIPTION MADE EASY

TURN AROUND TIME

We offer a standard turnaround of 3 – 4 business days, depending on the size of the order. We also offer 24-hour and 48-hour turnarounds but additional fees apply. View Pricing for more details.

TIME STAMPS

If you require time-stamping on your transcripts, be sure to let us know when you’re getting in touch. There is a small additional fee of €0.15/per audio minute for time stamps.

ACCENTS

If you are concerned about heavily accented speakers within the audio material, don’t worry, we hire only highly experienced and fully vetted transcriptionists who have dealt with most accents.

FORMATTING

The formatting used for every transcript is very clean, clear and professional. At the beginning of each transcript, there is a portion for the file details, i.e., title, length of audio, number of speakers, and audio quality. For two-speaker audio files, the speakers are denoted as interviewer/interviewee or speaker 1/speaker 2. For audio files with more than two speakers, the interviewer is denoted with bold and italic font and the interviewee is denoted with standard font. If you would like a more specific format, let us know and send in a template when placing the order.

FILE TYPES

We accept mostly all media formats including: MP3, MP4, WMV, AIF, M4A, MOV, AVI, VOB, AMR, WMA, OGG, and WAV.

CONFIDENTIALITY

We understand that confidentiality is very important for some of our clients. We ensure that all of your work is kept confidential by having every member of our team sign a confidentiality agreement (NDA) before coming on board. We also encourage our transcriptionists to immediately delete audio material once they have finished transcribing and proofreading it.
